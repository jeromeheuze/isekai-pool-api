About isekai-pool.com

What

Public RPC infrastructure for CPU-mineable yespower and yescrypt coins — so wallets, scripts, and miners can talk to the network without everyone running a full node at home.

Why

Small coins often lose reliable public endpoints. This project keeps a few networks reachable: free JSON-RPC through the API, documented for builders and home miners.

Who

Run by a solo developer. Not a company, not a pool (yet) — just infrastructure.

Uptime

Best-effort. There is no SLA. Check Node Status for live health.
